About me

I'm a Chartered Accountant based in Raipur, currently working in the Finance Vertical at Batra Deepak and Associates, where I spend most of my time on project financing, credit appraisal, financial projections, and regulatory filings across sectors like healthcare, ethanol & biofuels, and real estate.
